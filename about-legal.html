About the Chronicle

The Porkyville Chronicle is the official national newspaper of the Piggian Empire. We report on events across the Overworld, Nether, and End — from politics and economics to culture and world affairs. Our mission is to keep citizens informed with timely, accurate, and engaging news coverage.

Editorial Standards

Articles published in the Chronicle are prepared by our newsroom staff and contributing reporters across the realms. While satire, fictional events, and in-universe reporting are part of our coverage, we strive to clearly distinguish between factual updates and creative storytelling.
